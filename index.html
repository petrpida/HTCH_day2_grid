Spicy Plum Marinade

Author: Michal Pstross

Do you love meat combined with sweet sauces? Then you have to try the marinade from plum and orange marmalade. There is also chilli pepper in it, which gives it a pleasant taste tip. Start grilling and impress your friends whit new flavours

  • Prep time: 5 minutes
  • Difficulty: easy

Ingredients

(1 serving)
  • 12 pieces of plum or jam
  • 1 cup of orange juice
  • 1 red chilli pepper
  • 1/2 cup of orange marmalade

Procedure

  1. If we have fresh plums, finely chop them
  2. Crush the pepper
  3. Put everything in a pot, add orange juice and marmalade and cook slowly for about half an hour
  4. brush the meat with the marinade while grilling
